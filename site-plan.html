Purpose

Hi, I am a student, project manager and mother of two young children. My life can get pretty busy, how about yours? I know that I am not the only parent who is juggling many roles. Keeping piano lessons, soccer practice or pajama day at school straight can be overwhelming. That is why I am creating a site designed for parents who need to organize their lives and schedules to include their kids. One place that can track when it is, where it is and other important details. Come! Use a scheduler that is designed by parents for parents.

Target Audience

The target audience for my Parents & Plans site is parents who have to juggle one or more kids schedules along with their own. Many parents, including myself, find themselves in multiple roles and can feel overwhelmed keeping everyone’s plans straight. This site for parents provides a place to track all of their kids' schedule needs, including type, location and time details. As a busy parent it is comforting to know that all the things you need to remember for your child's extracurricular, school, church activities, etc. are organized and easily accessible. The stress of keeping track of everything you need to as a parent and fulfilling your other responsibilities can be alleviated. Find more peace of mind by using the Parents & Plans site.

Home page

Mission

The mission of Planned, Parents & Plans is to create a better way to manage parenting responsibilities with other life responsibilities. This means that parents have a place to input, view and organize events and activities for their children. Scheduling is more than a day on a calendar, we want parents to feel like they have a handle on what is happening. We want it to be clear and manageable. Getting organized is a great way to alleviate stress, so let’s get to it!

Easy Does It

To make things easier we have included a search bar and categories section so you can quickly filter by child, activity category, location or date. Every detail is at your fingertips while being presented to you as simply as needed.

You Can Do This!

This site is made for parents, by parents. After going a little batty from being a full-time parent, student, and intern, we figured that we aren’t the only ones looking for a bit more sanity and clarity when it comes to managing our kids' activities. Rest assured we made this with you in mind, or rather us in mind, so that should be even better. Consider us your new personal cheerleaders. You can do this! And we can help!

Plans

Step 1: Child

At Planned we understand that you may have one child or many. Maybe you are taking care of children that aren’t your own- like a nanny or opare. Whatever your family style is, we want you to feel confident your child’s scheduling needs are being met. One of the first steps to creating a plan is to input the child’s information. You may put as little as a first and last name, or you can add in important home info like address and phone number (this can be handy for carpooling situations). Once your child is added to the plan, you can start getting things on the schedule

Step 2: Schedule

We know that every second of your time is valuable. That’s why we have created a pain free method to add activities to the schedule. When you input an activity you can select the category, location, and activity name in a flash. This way when you are managing your little ones schedule you can easily access where and when things are happening.

Step 3: Breathe

Take a deep breath. You did it! You just started on the path to a more organized life. Remember that this tool is just that, a tool for you to be your best self. You can be a good parent, employee, co-worker, and anything else. Prioritize, organize and get Planned.

About Us

Jessica Reece, Founder

I am an Intern, full-time student and mother. Busy doesn’t even scratch the surface. In an effort to feel more confident in my daily parenting efforts, I created Planned. Planned is a planner specifically for parents, to help get your kids schedules organized. It is customizable and easy to use. I can quickly view my day, week, or month as whole, or filter by individual child, activity, location, etc.I won’t always be an intern and in school, but I know that I will always be juggling parenting with personal life. I am grateful for a tool to help me do that. I hope you enjoy it too!
