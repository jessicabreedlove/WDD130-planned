About Us

Jessica Reece, Founder

I am an Intern, full-time student and mother. Busy doesn’t even scratch the surface. In an effort to feel more confident in my daily parenting efforts, I created Planned. Planned is a planner specifically for parents, to help get your kids schedules organized. It is customizable and easy to use. I can quickly view my day, week, or month as whole, or filter by individual child, activity, location, etc.I won’t always be an intern and in school, but I know that I will always be juggling parenting with personal life. I am grateful for a tool to help me do that. I hope you enjoy it too!
